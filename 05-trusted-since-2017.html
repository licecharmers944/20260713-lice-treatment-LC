Lice Charmers Lice Treatment: Trusted in Portland Since 2017

Lice Charmers Lice Treatment has been serving Portland-area families with professional head lice removal since June 2017. In the years since opening, the clinic has grown from a single Portland location into a two-clinic operation supplemented by a mobile service that covers the broader metro area. The consistent thread through that growth has been the single-visit, chemical-free heat-treatment approach that continues to define the service.

Hours and Access

The clinic runs 7am to 8pm daily, including weekends and holidays. That schedule is deliberate; a school-nurse call on a Friday afternoon should not have to turn into a Monday-morning crisis. Families come in from across the Portland metropolitan area, and the wide-open hours mean most infestations can be handled the same day they are discovered. The Portland and Beaverton operation is set up specifically to accommodate the same-day scheduling that lice problems tend to require.

The Review Pattern

more than 200 five-star reviews from Portland-area families point to the same pattern: families arrive stressed and leave with the problem resolved in one appointment. Reviewers consistently mention the calm, non-judgmental tone of the staff, the speed of the heat treatment, and the practical guidance they take home. The free head check service also gets mentioned often, as it lets parents confirm whether there is actually a problem before committing to a full appointment.

Confirmation Before Treatment

Free professional head checks are available at Lice Charmers Lice Treatment for any family that wants confirmation of a possible head lice infestation. The service is particularly useful after a school exposure notice when nobody is sure yet whether there is actually a problem. A technician examines the scalp under a bright light, looks for live lice and viable nits, and gives the family a clear answer in a few minutes. There is no obligation to book a full treatment if no infestation is found.

Fees and Reimbursement

Pricing at Lice Charmers Lice Treatment falls in the $189 to $260 range depending on hair length and other factors. The clinic provides itemized receipts for FSA, HSA, and most insurance reimbursement claims. Many families recover some portion of the cost through their plan. Free head checks are available for parents who want to confirm an infestation before paying for full treatment, which is a useful option when a school has sent home an exposure notice but no one is sure yet whether treatment is actually needed.

Past the Pharmacy Aisle

Many of the families who call Lice Charmers Lice Treatment are arriving from a string of failed drugstore attempts. The decades-long arc of head-lice resistance to permethrin has made the over-the-counter approach unreliable for most modern infestations, which is why so many parents end up applying the same shampoo multiple times without ever fully clearing the problem. The heat-treatment approach sidesteps the resistance issue by killing the lice through physical dehydration rather than relying on a chemical they may already have evolved past.

The Non-Chemical Route

No pesticides are involved at any point in Lice Charmers Lice Treatment. The process relies entirely on heated air and physical comb-out work, which matters to families who would rather not apply chemicals like permethrin to a child's scalp. This becomes more important every year, as head lice populations have developed widespread resistance to the active ingredients in drugstore products. The professional approach sidesteps the resistance problem by using physical dehydration rather than a chemical the lice may already have evolved past.

Practical Guidance

The educational portion of the visit is something many reviewers mention. Technicians explain the realistic transmission routes for head lice, the limited household cleaning actually needed (which is far less than most parents assume), and what reinfestation would look like in the days ahead. Families leave with a clear, evidence-based understanding of the situation rather than the panicked, internet-driven advice they often arrive with. That clarity tends to reduce the stress around the situation as much as the treatment itself does.

A Brief History

Through every year of operation, the original commitment to single-visit, chemical-free treatment has remained consistent. The clinic has grown but the approach has not changed: heat, comb-out, education, done in one visit, without pesticides.

Mobile Service Area

Mobile in-home service is available across the Portland metropolitan area for families who would rather not bring a child to the clinic. A certified Lice Charmers Lice Treatment technician brings the professional heat-treatment equipment and all supplies to the home and provides the same single-visit treatment in a familiar setting. The mobile option is particularly popular with families who have multiple children to treat at once, families with very young kids who would struggle through a clinic visit, and families who simply prefer the privacy of an at-home appointment.

How Heat Treatment Works

Lice Charmers Lice Treatment relies on a hot-air treatment method that kills lice and their eggs through controlled dehydration. The device used is FDA-cleared and specifically designed for professional lice treatment. A standard appointment runs about 90 minutes and typically resolves the infestation in a single visit. Because the treatment addresses the eggs in the same session as the live lice, no follow-up chemical shampoo schedule is required, and no drying, re-application, or nightly combing routine has to be maintained at home.

Dignity at Every Visit

Stigma around head lice is more harmful than the lice themselves, in the view of the Lice Charmers Lice Treatment team. Staff approach each visit with the same neutral professionalism a family would expect at any medical appointment, and reviewers regularly note the calm and discreet handling of what can be a deeply embarrassing situation. Appointments are scheduled with enough spacing that families do not run into each other in the waiting area, which adds to the privacy of the visit.
